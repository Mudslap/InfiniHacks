about

chickenmath is a short math test, featuring a variety of different basic problems that can teach anyone to be better at math on a quicker time or just strengthen your knowledge all together.


Originally created by Vincent Aquino, chickenmath is determined to provide the nighest quality equations that everyone can use to learn more.


Math Problems

Right now, our math problems are confined to only basic addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. Our team is hoping to change that and add more problems to fit a more diverse environment.


Settings

If you wish to change your focus on what tye of equations appear, you can easily use your settings to change that. Settings can also be used to change the time of the test.


Bugs/Reporting
